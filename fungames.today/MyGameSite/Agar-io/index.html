Game Overview

This is a classic and highly optimized multiplayer online arena (IO) game. In this microscopic world, the only rule of survival is "eat or be eaten." Players control a cell and must grow larger by devouring colorful particles on the map and smaller cells, while constantly watching out for giant predators.

Featuring a smooth physics engine and smart AI opponents, it offers a stress-relieving yet thrilling competitive experience anytime, anywhere.
