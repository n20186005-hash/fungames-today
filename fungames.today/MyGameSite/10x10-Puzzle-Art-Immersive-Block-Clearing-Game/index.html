Description

Welcome to 10x10 Puzzle Art! This is a minimalist and relaxing puzzle game designed to challenge your spatial reasoning. On a 10x10 board, strategically place blocks to clear lines and chase the highest score!

Features stunning visuals with neon colors and satisfying particle effects when you clear lines.

How to Play

  • Drag & Drop: Drag blocks from the bottom to the board.
  • Clear Lines: Fill a row or column completely to clear blocks and earn points.
  • Game Over: Plan wisely! The game ends if there's no space for new blocks.
  • Combo: Clear multiple lines at once for bonus points!
