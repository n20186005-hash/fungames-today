Gulper.io - Neon Snake Battle

Game Overview

Welcome to Gulper.io, an immersive cyberpunk-style multiplayer arcade game. In this dark digital world pulsating with neon lights, you control a glowing energy snake. Your goal is simple: slither through the void, consume energy dots to grow, and outmaneuver opponents to dominate the leaderboard.

Whether you are a competitive player seeking high-speed action or a casual player looking to relax, the dual game modes offer something for everyone.

âœ¨ Key Features

  • Stunning Neon Visuals: High-contrast fluorescent colors and dynamic lighting effects, creating a cool sci-fi atmosphere.
  • Dual Game Modes:
    - Standard Mode: The classic survival challenge. Hitting walls or other snakes results in immediate game over.
    - Infinite Mode: An "immortal" experience. You bounce off walls and other snakes instead of dying, perfect for practicing skills or relaxing.
  • Smart Save System: Auto-save function included. If you accidentally close the tab, click "Resume" to restore your length and score.
  • Immersive Audio: Equipped with dynamic Synth sound effects that provide satisfying feedback when boosting or scoring.
