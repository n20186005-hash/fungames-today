Brawl: Apple Snatch

Developer: Independent Game Developer

Description

Brawl: Apple Snatch is a fast-paced, retro-pixel style casual competitive game. In this colorful world, your goal is simple: eat more apples than your opponent! The game tests not only your reaction speed but also your strategic use of terrain and body size mechanics to knock your opponents flying.

Whether you're challenging the high-IQ computer AI solo or battling it out with a friend on the same screen, this is a party game guaranteed to bring sweaty palms and endless laughter.

How to Play?

  1. Select Mode: Choose Single Player (vs AI) or Two Players (Local Co-op).
  2. Snatch Apples: Delicious red apples fall from the sky. Eating them increases your score and makes your character BIGGER!
  3. Avoid Poison: Watch out for purple poison apples! Eating one deducts score, makes you smaller, and leaves you vulnerable.
  4. Attack & Push: Use your size advantage to ram or attack opponents, stunning them or knocking them off the map for a huge advantage.
  5. Win: The player with the highest score when the timer ends wins!

Gameplay Mechanics

The core lies in "Scramble" and "Survival". It's not just a collection game; you must watch your opponent. By continuously eating red apples, your character's size increases significantly, giving you stronger pushing power to knock smaller opponents off cliffs. However, being big also means you are an easier target for poison apples and attacks.

Key Features

  • Size Changing Mechanism: Eat more to get heavier and stronger; get poisoned to become light and easily knocked away.
  • Dynamic Maps: From the Classic Pyramid to Dangerous Floating Islands and the Walled Arena.
  • Physical Comedy: Watching an opponent fly off-screen from a punch or fall into the abyss due to greed is always hilarious.
  • Local Multiplayer: One device, two friends, infinite fun. No internet needed for PvP.

Compatibility

No download required. Works perfectly on:

  • PC / Mac: Best experience for 2-player local battles.
  • Mobile / Tablet: Optimized touch controls for on-the-go play.
