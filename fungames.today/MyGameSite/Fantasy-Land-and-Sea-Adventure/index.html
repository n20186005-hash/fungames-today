Fantasy Ocean Adventure

Indie Dev Designed & Developed by wu shu cheng - Inspired by hand-drawn art.

Game Overview

Welcome to Fantasy Ocean Adventure! This is a relaxing and healing deep-sea exploration game. In this wonderful world made of crayon touches, the sky is bright blue, and mysterious bubble cities sit on the seabed.

You play as a submarine pilot, driving the iconic yellow submarine to shuttle between the sparkling sea surface and the deep sea.

âœ¨ Key Features

  • Hand-Drawn Style: Perfectly restores the mushroom houses, dolphins, UFOs, and bubble cities from the original artwork.
  • Dynamic Environment: Watch waves undulate, bubbles rise, dolphins jump, and UFOs hover in the sky.
  • Immersive Audio: Enjoy the crisp sound of collecting fish and the bubbling noise when surfacing.
  • Resource Management: Keep an eye on your Oxygen! Surface to refill or risk mission failure.
