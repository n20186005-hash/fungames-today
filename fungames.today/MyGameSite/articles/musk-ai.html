Musk Announces League of Legends AI Showdown

Nov 26, 2025 Esports & AI Source: Esports Insider

Elon Musk announced via social media platform X that a five-game showdown between top global League of Legends professional players and Grok 5 AI will be held in 2026.

According to the announcement, Grok 5 will utilize advanced computer vision, collecting screen images solely via cameras to ensure fairness and mimic human visual input constraints.

T1, the winner of the 2025 Global Championship, has already responded with willingness to participate in this historic event. This match is expected to spark significant attention across both the esports and artificial intelligence industries, potentially setting a new benchmark for AI capabilities in complex strategic environments.

More details regarding the venue and specific rules will be released in the coming months.
