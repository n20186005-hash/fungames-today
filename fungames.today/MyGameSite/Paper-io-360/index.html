Game Overview

This is a color war of speed, strategy, and guts! In this minimalist arena, you play as a moving block, drawing lines to claim and expand your territory. Your goal is simple: conquer as much of the map as possible, defeat competitors, and dominate the leaderboard.

But bewareâ€”when you leave your territory, the trail you leave behind is your fatal weak spot. If an opponent cuts through it, it's game over! With optimized controls and the thoughtful addition of "Immortal Mode," it caters to both hardcore players and casual players looking to relax.

âœ¨ Key Features

  • Dual Mode Experience:
    - Classic Mode: Experience the authentic tension. Hitting walls or having your trail cut results in instant death.
    - Immortal Mode (Infinite): Designed for stress relief. You bounce off walls, and attacks only clear your trail without killing you.
  • Smart Auto-Save: Features a built-in auto-save function. Resume your progress anytime by clicking "Continue."
  • Immersive Experience: Equipped with vivid sound effects (capture, kill, defeat) and supports one-click muting.
  • Real-Time Data: Features a mini-map, real-time territory percentage bar, and dynamic leaderboard.
